# Organic Planning Prompt Addendum — Timeframe KPI V7

Campaign timeframe is mandatory.

Read from Page_Channel_Library:
start_day
end_day
duration
current_followers
current_likes
target_followers
target_likes
primary_growth_metric
growth_goal

Before creating a content plan, calculate:
days_elapsed
days_remaining
campaign_progress_percent
follower_gap
like_gap
required_followers_per_day
required_likes_per_day
kpi_status
content_intensity
future_queue_needed

If KPI is behind:
increase humor, relatable, shareable emotional, identity, light debate, hot_take, self_recognition, comment triggers, tagging prompts.

Reduce:
low-reaction product-heavy posts
overly soft vague emotional posts
generic education without interaction

Return campaign_kpi_context in output.
