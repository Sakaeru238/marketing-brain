FACEBOOK IMAGE STANDARD:

For Facebook feed organic images, default to:
- 4:5 vertical aspect ratio
- 1080 x 1350 px or 1440 x 1800 px
- JPG/PNG-style static image
- mobile-first composition
- main subject centered and readable in feed
- minimal text overlay
- natural organic Facebook post style, not a hard-sell ad layout

Avoid:
- excessive text in image
- fake logos or fake product packaging
- discount badges unless product_mention_level is direct
- hard-sell CTA text
- cluttered compositions
- tiny unreadable details
- unrealistic hands, labels, screens, or packaging
